Hvad laver jeg?

I love great ideas, which is why creative thinking is the foundation of my work. My main goal is to make creative work that make people interact and react.

I'm a freelance photographer, filmmaker and Photoshop geek, specialising in creative concepts. Through visual communication I create photos and videos with meaningful stories and custom aesthetics.

I'm currently working freelance as a photographer and videographer at Laptop Club (the photo studio I co-founded) while producing commercial photo and video content for MediaCom Beyond Advertising.

Hvem er jeg?

My name is Sonja Lovdal and I am 22 years old. I grew up in Aalborg, Denmark and now live and work in Copenhagen.

I started working professionally with photography and other creative content when I was only 14 years old. Ever since then, the projects have gotten bigger and I have gotten more and more inspired to do awesome and fun work. I love what I do!

I've also attended some pretty awesome photography programs at School of Creative and Performing Arts in New York, MediaCollege, Danish School of Media- and Journalism and Engelsholm Folk High School among others.
